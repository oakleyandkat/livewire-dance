# Livewire Daily

A daily training and progress tracker for the Livewire dance team.

## How to use

**Open `index.html` in your browser** — just double-click the file in Finder.

That's it. No install, no setup. The app runs entirely in your browser and saves your progress on your device.

## What it does

- **Countdown to Nationals** — big number, front and center
- **Practices remaining** — admin can update
- **Daily training checklist** — all 4 sections from the team handout (Warm-Up, Flexibility, Strength + Control, Feet + Point Work)
- **Tap any exercise** to expand it and see the "how to do it" cues and reminders
- **Daily reset** — checks clear at midnight automatically (or hit the "Reset today" button)
- **Streak counter** — counts consecutive days you finished the full checklist
- **Two roles** — Dancers see their own checklist. Admins also get a panel to set the Nationals date and practices count.

## Privacy

Each dancer's progress is saved in their own browser's storage on their own device. Nobody else can see it.
